Our story

A black-led contractor with a track record the parastatals trust.

Founded in 2000 and led by Managing Director Dr. Gabriel Bonginkosi Ngomane, TandZ Construction has grown from a single electrical contract into a multi-discipline construction business delivering across Mpumalanga, Limpopo, Gauteng and beyond.

Flat structure. Experienced functional heads. Direct communication. That's why our clients come back — and why their projects finish on time.

Our Mission

At T&Z Construction CC, our mission reflects a deep commitment to making a positive, lasting impact on the lives of South Africans by excelling in the design, provision and maintenance of civil engineering infrastructure. We strive to be a beacon of reliability and proficiency in delivering top-notch solutions for both the public and private sectors — roads, bridges, buildings and other vital structures, built to last.

Our Vision

Management has a lot to do with answers. Leadership is a function of questions. And the first question for a leader is always: "Who do we intend to be?" Not "what are we going to do?" — but who we intend to be for our clients, our teams and the communities we build for.
